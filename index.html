About Pi INU

Pi INU is the first meme token inspired by the Pi Network, operating on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Our mission is to combine the philosophy of the Pi Network with the speed and liquidity of the BSC environment, creating a gateway for millions of pioneers to explore the DeFi world.

Historical Milestone:

During the initial listing on the Decoin exchange, Pi INU witnessed explosive growth, driven by an overwhelming majority of Japanese investors. This early support established a rock-solid foundation for our global community.
