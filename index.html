Pi INU is a meme-inspired crypto project launched in November 2021, rooted in the global Pi Network community and driven by strong and sustained interest from users worldwide.

While Pi INU and the Pi Core Team follow different operational paths, both share the broader Pioneer ecosystem. As independent entities, they continue to evolve separately—yet remain connected through a shared vision and community foundation.

Moving forward, Pi INU positions itself as both a constructive competitor and a potential collaborator within this expanding landscape.

Since its inception, Pi INU has continuously explored diverse approaches to ecosystem development. Through real-world experience and iteration, the project has built its own operational expertise and achieved meaningful technical progress.

Unlike short-term pump-and-dump meme tokens, Pi INU represents a long-term brand with independent value, sustainability, and a clear direction.

Over the years, Pi INU has developed and expanded its ecosystem through DEX & CEX listings, a mining application, blockchain games, a cross-chain swap system, NFTs, and staking services.

The project has gained recognition through global media coverage including Benzinga, Coinpost, and BSCN, and has collaborated with IoTeX Korea, Pi Store Korea, and TELTLK.

Pi INU has completed smart contract audits for both PINU and PINU100X, is listed on CoinMarketCap, and operates under a registered LLC in New Mexico, USA.

The community continues to grow across platforms including X, Telegram, YouTube, TikTok, Kakao, Discord, and Facebook.
